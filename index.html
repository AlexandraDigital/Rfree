Impact Lab

Knowledge should move outward into the world.

The Impact Lab helps learners use what they study to solve real problems in their homes, neighborhoods, schools, and communities.

Step 1

Learn a real-world topic

Build practical understanding in finance, health, technology, climate, or civic life.

Step 2

Find a local challenge

Map the issue that matters, from misinformation to food access to youth opportunity.

Step 3

Create a project

Design a workshop, toolkit, campaign, business, prototype, or neighborhood solution.

Step 4

Share the impact

Track outcomes, showcase wins, and build confidence through visible contribution.

How RFree Works

Three communities make the academy possible.

Learners

Access tuition-free programs, live classes, guided projects, and a supportive learning network.

Teachers

Lead flagship courses, host workshops, mentor learners, or contribute expertise in focused sessions.

Funders

Support cohorts, sponsor teachers, underwrite Impact Lab projects, and expand access sustainably.

Funding Model

Free for learners. Sustainable by design.

RFree Academy uses a blended funding model so access does not depend on a learner's income.

Monthly donors

Recurring support keeps core classes free and gives the academy stable operating momentum.

Cohort sponsors

Foundations, companies, and local partners fund specific learner groups and measurable outcomes.

Mission-aligned workshops

Paid training for organizations subsidizes tuition-free education for underserved learners.

Grants and major gifts

Strategic philanthropy helps expand curriculum, teacher support, and community impact capacity.

Teach with RFree

People with lived experience, professional skill, and teaching heart belong here.

Teaching can be flexible, supported, and deeply meaningful. We are building pathways for both volunteer contributors and paid lead instructors.

Ways to contribute

  • Teach a full learning track
  • Host a single workshop
  • Lead office hours or mentoring
  • Design project prompts for the Impact Lab

What teachers receive

  • Mission-aligned visibility
  • Flexible time commitments
  • Supportive learner community
  • Stipends when funding allows
