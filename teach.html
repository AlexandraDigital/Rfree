Teach with RFree

People with lived experience, professional skill, and teaching heart belong here.

Teaching can be flexible, supported, and deeply meaningful. RFree Academy is building pathways for both volunteer contributors and paid lead instructors.

Ways to contribute

  • Teach a full learning track
  • Host a single workshop
  • Lead office hours or mentoring
  • Design project prompts for the Impact Lab

What teachers receive

  • Mission-aligned visibility
  • Flexible time commitments
  • Supportive learner community
  • Stipends when funding allows

Next Step

The studio is where teaching becomes a real course.

Once someone is ready to contribute, the studio gives them a place to build or update the actual course experience.
